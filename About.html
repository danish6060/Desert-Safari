About UAE Trip Desert Safari

Our Story

UAE Trip was founded with one simple goal — to deliver the most authentic, safe, and exhilarating Desert Safari Dubai experiences for travelers from all around the world. As a locally managed booking platform, our mission is to showcase the true beauty of the Arabian Desert while offering seamless online booking and world-class service.

From thrilling dune bashing adventures in powerful 4x4 vehicles to serene camel rides under the golden sunset, UAE Trip has been connecting explorers to the heart of the desert for over a decade. Our expert team of licensed guides, drivers, and hospitality professionals ensures that every safari we organize leaves you with unforgettable memories and a deep connection to Dubai’s rich cultural heritage.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate travel experts and UAE locals who believe that the desert is not just a landscape — it’s an experience. Based in Dubai, UAE Trip operates as an official tourism and adventure booking agency specializing in desert safaris, dune buggy rides, camel trekking, and premium evening tours.

Each of our packages is carefully curated to meet diverse traveler preferences — whether you’re an adventure seeker, a romantic couple, or a family looking for a unique experience. With flexible booking options, instant confirmations, and 24/7 support, we make it easy to plan your perfect desert day.
